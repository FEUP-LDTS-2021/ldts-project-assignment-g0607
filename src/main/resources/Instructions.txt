        ***********fFROGGER GAME RULESf***********
-YOU WILL START THE GAME WITH 3 LIVES AND THE FIRST FROG
IS SHOWN ON THE SIDEWALK BELOW THE TRAFFIC.
-PRESS THE ARROW KEYS UP, DOWN, LEFT AND RIGHT TO HOP
IN THOSE DIRECTIONS.
-YOU WILL ONLY GO TO THE NEXT LEVEL IF YOU MANAGE TO
GET 3 FROGS TO THEIR HOUSE WITHOUT LOSING THE 3 LIVES.
-AFTER YOU'VE FINISHED THE GAME, YOU CAN PRESS ENTER
TO GO BACK TO THE MENU.
-PRESS q or Q TO QUIT THE GAME.
-HAVE FUN!!!
